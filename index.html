Anatomical Diagrams of the Head and Neck

by Alex He

This is a small study aid that I intend to keep updating as a project. It contains labelled simplified diagrams I have drawn that show some essential head and neck components.

This site functions best on a laptop (no mobile support) and is interactive (canvas can zoom in, buttons can be ticked)

Please be aware I cannot guarantee the accuracy of any drawings or spellings used.
