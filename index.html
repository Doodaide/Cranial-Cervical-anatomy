This is a small study aid that I intend to keep updating as a project. It contains labelled simplified diagrams I have drawn that show some essential head and neck components. Many of these are made from composites of illustrated diagrams found in Netter's, or tracings of images found online. I intend to add functionality to them by either adding labels, overlapping or collating similar images to show more thorough depictions of the anatomy. Thus, much of the work here isn't strictly original

However, I try my best to at least add my own labels or modify the images as best I can.

This site functions best on a laptop (no mobile support) and is interactive (canvas can zoom in, buttons can be ticked)

Please be aware I cannot guarantee the accuracy of any drawings or spellings used mainly because I am sometimes a buffoon and I don't use spellcheck on VsCode.

If you have anything you wish to update me on, request, or inform me about mistakes, please send me an email at: business.alexander.he@gmail.com
